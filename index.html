GRE Practice Tool

The Graduate Record Examinations (GRE) is a standardised test that is an admissions requirement for most graduate schools in the United States. The General Test comprises of three components: analytical writing, quantitative reasoning, and verbal reasoning.

  • Vocabulary Quiz

    • This app is useful to recap academic vocabularies for GRE Verbal Section.

  • GRE Writing App

    • GRE Writing App offered at Tu's Studio simulates the writing component of GRE General Test, adopting Official Pool of Writing Prompts publicly provided by ETS. This simulation tool allows candidate practice under time constraint in actual paper (30 minutes). Auto spelling correction is absent from this app writing editor to simulate the actual testing software.

    For more information about the test, please visit ETS Website.
